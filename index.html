Everything is Figureoutable

A MANTRA TO LIVE BY



I have been at a crossroads lately, which has forced me to think about my life and the paths I have taken. As a mother of 3, I found myself lost in the mom role and wanting more. I came across this book Everyting is Figureoutable by Marie Forleo through my career coach. And from the moment I picked up this book, I find that I am more energetic, more alive, and more equipped to take on the world outside of motherhood. I encourage anyone looking for a change, whether it be in a career, in a relationship, in life to read this book and experience what is feels like to see that everything has a solution and to simply have a more positive outlook.
